  8. click: create debate button and wait until the page says debate created. ~30 seconds

  10. This is the debate: you will select a movie to remove 5 times until there is only 1 left which is your "chosen" movie. When you remove a movie
  11. once you have one movie left. you can go back to the Rank Movies page.
  12. click: move to next user trial round (then it will tell you what round you are on)
  13. repeat steps 8-12 until you have completed the 6 rounds. Once you are on round 6. complete the debate then you are done.
